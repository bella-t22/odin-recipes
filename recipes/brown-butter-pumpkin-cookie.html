Brown Butter Pumpkin Snickerdoodles

Description

Soft, chewy, and spiced to perfection, these brown butter pumpkin cookies are the fall cookie of your dreams!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Butter
  • 1/3 cup Pumpkin Puree
  • 1/2 cup Brown Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 1 Egg Yolk
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 1/4 cup Flour
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/2 tsp Cream of Tartar
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1/8 tsp Nutmeg

Cinnamon Sugar Coating

  • 1/3 cup Sugar
  • 1 tbsp Cinnamon
  • Pinch of Salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Set aside.
  2. Combine all ingredients for cinnamon sugar mixture and set aside.
  3. Place pumpkin puree on a thick paper towel and thoroughly dry it out by gently squeezing out the moisture.
  4. In a large bowl, combine melted butter, pumpkin puree, and sugars. Whisk to combine.
  5. Add egg yolk and vanilla extract and mix well.
  6. Add the flour, baking soda, cream of tartar, salt, and spices. Mix until a soft dough forms and no streaks of flour remain.
  7. Scoop dough into small balls and coat them in cinnamon sugar mixture. Set them on the baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.
  8. Enjoy!
