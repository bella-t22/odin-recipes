Earl Grey Tea Cake

Description

This scrumptious delight deserves to be eaten pinky out!

This cake takes an average of 1 hour 30 minutes to make.

Ingredients

Earl Grey Tea Cake

  • 2/3 cup Milk
  • 5 Earl Grey Tea Bags
  • 7 oz Unsalted Butter
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 3 Eggs
  • 2 cups Flour
  • 1 tsp Lemon Zest
  • 1 1/3 tsp Baking Powder

Earl Grey Glaze

  • 1 1/2 cups Powdered Sugar
  • 3/4 cup Butter
  • 3-4 tbsp Earl Grey Tea

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. In a medium bowl whisk flour, baking powder, and tea until well combined.
  3. Use a standing mixer to cream butter and sugar together until pale and fluffy. Reduce speed and add eggs, lemon zest, and vanilla.
  4. Add milk to butter and sugar mixture.
  5. Slowly add flour mixture into wet mixture and mix until combined.
  6. Pour mixture into a greased pan and bake for 35 minutes.
  7. While this is cooking, make glaze by combining all ingredients and mix using standing mixer on high for 3 minutes.
  8. Add icing to cake once it is done baking.
