Poppy Seed Muffins

Description

These poppy seed muffins are a great way to start your day! They are buttery, soft, sweet, and will make you say, "Mmmmm"!

These muffins are easy to prepare and can be made in as little as 30 minutes. Let's get started!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup Milk
  • 1 1/4 cups Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Canola Oil
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tbsp Poppy Seeds
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 tsp Almond Extract
  • 1 1/2 cups Flour
  • 3/4 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees and line a muffin tin with muffin liners.
  2. In a large bowl whisk together milk, sugar, oil eggs, poppy seeds, vanilla extract, and almond extract.
  3. Sift together flour, baking powder, and salt. Then, add to the wet ingredients and whisk until combined.
  4. Scoop batter into muffin liners.
  5. Bake for 20-22 minutes.
